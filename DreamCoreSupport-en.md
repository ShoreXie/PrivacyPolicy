 ## ‚ùì FAQs
 
 ### Shooting
 
 **Q: Why do RAW photos look darker?**
 A: RAW preserves the original sensor data and requires post-processing to achieve the best results. You can use in-app filters or export to professional editing software.
 
 ### Time Capsule Blind Box
 
 **Q: What is the Time Capsule Blind Box?**
 A: It randomly selects one of the photos you've taken in this app as the background to create a unique double-exposure effect.
 
 **Q: Is there a limit on background changes?**
 A: In the free version, each photo can change its background up to 3 times. Premium members enjoy unlimited changes.
 
 ## üîß Troubleshooting
 
 ### App crashes or lags
 1. **Restart the app**: Fully close the app and reopen it
 2. **Restart the device**: Power cycle your iPhone/iPad
 3. **Check storage**: Ensure there is sufficient free space on the device
 4. **Update the app**: Check the App Store for updates
 
 ### Photos cannot be saved
 1. **Check permissions**: Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Photos, ensure DreamCore Camera has access
 2. **Storage space**: Ensure enough free space to save photos
 3. **Re-authorize**: Delete and reinstall the app, then grant permissions again
